The Alabaster Manor

Before (Bare Structure)
After (Handover)
Drag

Project Overview

Designed for a prominent business family in Guwahati, this high-end residential duplex is centered around architectural grandeur. We opened up structural partitions to build a double-height living room featuring custom alabaster gypsum wall panels and gold metal accents.

Our client requested a timeless layout that balances aesthetic weight with spacious light flows. We custom-manufactured all modular couches and dining arrays to blend into a premium cream, warm beige, and gold color canvas.

Client Requirements

Open spatial layout, double-height focus, warm gold integration.

Material Palette

Italian Statuario Marble, Brushed Brass trims, Cream Bouclé Fabric, Walnut veneer.

Execution Timeline

12 Weeks

Remodeling a closed, dated kitchen layout into a modern open culinary suite. We installed European fittings with soft-close tandem slides, custom quartz countertops, and dedicated appliance pockets.
