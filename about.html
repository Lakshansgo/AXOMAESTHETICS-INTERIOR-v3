The Aesthetic Legacy

AXOMAESTHETICS INTERIOR is a premier design house born in Guwahati, committed to shaping spaces of luxury and function.

Since 2016

Shaping Space,
Refining Lifestyles

AXOMAESTHETICS INTERIOR was established in 2016 in Guwahati, Assam, with a simple yet ambitious vision: to introduce world-class, creative-agency quality interior architecture to Northeast India.

We grew from a boutique design studio into the region's most sought-after luxury firm. Today, we stand as a full-service agency managing high-end residential estates, modern corporate offices, and custom spatial renovations.

Our work is characterized by refined palettes, clean lines, and custom details, creating interiors that represent silent grandeur rather than loud excess.

Creative Vision

Ananya Baruah

Principal Architect & Founder
"Luxury is not about filling a room with gold items. It is about spatial harmony, the touch of raw wood, the drop of light on a marble slab, and how the environment welcomes you home."

Ananya Baruah holds a degree in Architectural Design and has spent over a decade crafting elite residential layouts. Her design style bridges minimalist structural plans with rich texture styling.

Under her guidance, AXOMAESTHETICS has designed over 250 properties in Guwahati, Shillong, and Jorhat, establishing a recognized local standard for high-end spatial curation.
