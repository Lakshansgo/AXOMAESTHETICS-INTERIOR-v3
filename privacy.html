Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

At AXOMAESTHETICS INTERIOR, we prioritize the protection and confidentiality of our clients' personal data. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and process your coordinates and project files when utilizing our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you submit consultation booking forms or register for our design insights newsletter. This includes: name, contact number, email, project site location, estimated budget constraints, and custom design layouts notes.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to schedule appointments, evaluate design requests, tailor material boards, and coordinate structural execution tasks at your site. We do not sell or lease your personal coordinates to third-party marketing services.

3. Data Security

We implement electronic and procedural security layers to lock down site notes, budget documents, and 3D architectural renders. Access to your personal data is restricted solely to authorized execution partners and principal designers.

4. Contact Information
